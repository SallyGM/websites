Do you want to trek for charity but are put off by the costs?

We plan and organise the treks to cover costs but not to make a profit because we believe that as much money as possible should go to the charity.

For you, this means that there are no admin fees or hidden extras, and no minimum sponsorship. Our treks offer you an affordable opportunity to raise money for your chosen charity. Join with like-minded people to take on a challenge and raise money for your cause.

Our treks have relatively small numbers so that we can provide a very personal service and provide you with an unforgettable experience.

We organise treks in the Atlas mountains and also in the Sahara.

We are currently taking expressions of interest for our future treks to the Atlas Mountains and Sahara desert in Morocco and trips to Nepal. If you would like to take part or just receive further information when it becomes available, please contact us using the link on this page.

We look forward to hearing from you.

All of our treks include Moroccan transfers; insurance; all accommodation; all food whilst trekking; all breakfasts; local guides, chef, and mules whilst trekking; local taxes and tips; a donation of £50 to our chosen charities in Marrakech / Nepal.

You can also choose your own charity to raise money for—it’s up to you!
