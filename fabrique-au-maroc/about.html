Our story starts in February 2011 with an inspirational short break in Marrakech and the Atlas mountains. During this trip we fell in love with the sights and sounds of the city and surrounding countryside.

We were drawn to the beauty of the fabrics, the sabra silk with its spectacular jewel like threads produce a shimmering, sparkling, beautiful fabric.

We enjoyed spending time watching and chatting to the craftsmen in the souks and decided that we wanted to bring a taste of this inspiring culture back to the UK.

So we started a small business and regularly visit Morocco to hand pick items for our market stall. Over the years we have forged good relationships with the artists and sellers in the City.

We have now diversified and also organise treks to raise money for Moroccan charities.
